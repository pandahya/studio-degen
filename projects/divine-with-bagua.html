Divine with Bagua

2021

In this project, I created a digital divination tool with p5.js. It is based on Chinese Bagua and users can get their divination based on the Bagua symbol they chose.
The opening screen is a scene of Chinese Daoism temple, and the user will be able to get the divination by dragging on the screen.
Once the user picks a symbol and clicks "Get Fortune", the result of the divination will appear under the canvas.

Documentation

I don't have deep knowledge of Bagua either, but my interest in it drove me to research and learn about the basic representations of the symbols, which I later turned into this project.
It is a piece that gives the user a tiny touch on the mysterious Chinese Bagua.

It was really challenging to make the rotating wheel and get the fortune only to display after the button is clicked.

I also later turned this project in to a random picker in the same rotating wheel format.
