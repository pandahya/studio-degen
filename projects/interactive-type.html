Documentation

Interactive type is a series of projects that explore the intersection of typographic, colour, motion and user-interaction. They are colourful renderings of 4 letter words whose interactions are based on the meaning of the word.
For e.g., the word jump keeps bouncing around until it becomes illegible.

I refined three of my projects:
1. Fall: leaves slowly fall from the top of the screen forming the shape of the word. Additionally, moving the mouse over the leaves blows them around the screen and pressing the mouse down drops all the leaves

2. Atom: This mimicked molecule representations with the atoms slowly repelling one another. When the user places the mouse over an atom, the force of repulsion increases the spread.

3. Game: I tried to recreate the online game 'bounce ball.' All the rectangles move around the screen and the player has to move a slab (at the base of the screen) to redirect the rectangles back.

References

I relied on the Coding Train's video on Steering Behaviours to create the motion. I referenced examples from p5.js Generative Design Type in my wire project. For working with text, I refernced the following projects: textToPoints() Letter Morphing and classes and objects by xinxin
